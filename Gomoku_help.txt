Gomoku Help Menu
================

Objective:
- Be the first player to get five of your symbols in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally).

Game Rules:
1. Players alternate placing X (Player 1) and O (Player 2) on a 15Ã—15 grid
2. The first player to get an unbroken row of five symbols wins
3. If the board fills completely with no five-in-a-row, the game is a tie

Controls:
- Enter your move in format: <row> <column> (e.g., "8 15")
- 'undo' to undo your last move
- 'redo' to redo an undone move
- 'save' to save the game
- 'help' to display this help menu
